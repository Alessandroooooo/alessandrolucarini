Alessandro is an Italian graphic designer and visual artist based in the Netherlands. As an artist, his approach centres on embracing the unexpected and the beauty in visual mistakes. Alessandro is particularly interested in the power of visual storytelling to convey personal stories and experiences. Through his work, he explores themes of identity, memory, and social commentary with a balance between humour and romantic observations. He draws inspiration from his life experiences, cultural encounters, and current events. He's expressing his interests through the production of publications , videos and posters .
